Background

Sawadee kub everyone! I am Pason Cheam Tung Huay, a Chinese-Thai Malaysian currently a computer engineering student studying at UCSI University, Malaysia as of 2025.

Having my own website and creating programming tutorials as a side hustle has been on my bucket list for a long time ever since I was in secondary school. I always wanted to try to earn my own income while sharing knowledge with others. In the year 2025, I finally took the leap and made it happen! This website represents more than just a free programming tutorial platform, it is also a personal milestone that reflects my passion as a computer engineering student.

As a student, I wanted to push beyond the boundaries of the classroom by doing various projects that challenges me and applying my skills in real world scenarios. Building this site was entirely self-initiated, and it iss something I am truly proud of. Through this website, I hope to provide valuable learning resources while earning some honest cash.

My Milestones

Python workshop speaker

Another key milestone in my journey was conducting a hands on programming workshop where I introduced participants to fundamental programming concepts using Python. I covered essential topics such as user input, functions, lists, and loops, building a strong foundation for beginners to start their coding journey.

I also demonstrated how these basic concepts can be applied to solve real-world problems. For example, I guided the participants through a project that checks the validity of a credit card number and determines its type (e.g., VISA, MasterCard) using simple Python logic.

Interned at iFast

From September to December 2024, I had the incredible opportunity to work on real company projects as an intern at iFAST, contributing to full-stack development, including the FSMOne corporate action page. This experience allowed me to gain hands-on skills in Angular, Spring Framework, and API development, while deepening my understanding of software architecture and system integration.

Despite the challenges of working in a fast-paced fintech environment, I'm deeply grateful for the mentorship I received and the personal and professional growth it brought.

Member if IEEE

On 8th August 2024, I had the honor of attending and speaking at IEEE Night 2024, the first-ever event organized by UCSI IEEE HKN Mu Alpha. Being part of IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) has been a meaningful journey, not only for developing my professional and leadership skills, but also for building lasting friendships and connecting with like-minded peers in engineering.

During the event, I delivered an appreciation speech to recognize the top committee members, many of whom are my fellow engineering comrades, and surprised them with personalized lithophanes as a token of gratitude.

Competitions

I have participated invarious competitions, out of all of them, one of my proudest milestones was winning the championship at the UCSI Engineering Fair 2024 with Hazebuster, a real-time air monitoring, detection, and filtration system I developed.

This project challenged me to apply engineering principles and innovative thinking from early concept design to prototype development. It not only sharpened my technical skills but also reinforced my passion for building practical solutions that make a positive impact on society.
